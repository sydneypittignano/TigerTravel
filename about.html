ABOUT US


TigerTravel is an app to help Princeton students coordinate ridesharing to and from hotspots around campus. Whether it’s to lower the cost of rides or just to have some company while traveling, TigerTravel offers an effective, organized, user-friendly interface to help students to find others with similar travel plans to ride-share with.

TigerTravel was built by a project team as part of the course COS333: Advanced Programming Techniques. The class is taught by Bob Dondero, and these students were advised by Anat Klieman.
